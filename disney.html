Bodystorming

This was probably the main focus of this project. The most fun part, too. We started by deciding how we wanted our process to play out. After deciding the phases, we started creating the materials:

  • Helmet
  • Rocket
  • Various tasks
  • Planet introduction
  • Roleplaying script

    • After creating these things we then hosted our roleplaying astronaut experience as seen in the video above.

    RELFECTION

    Overall, this project was a lot of fun. A great blend of ideation and testing. It was fun deciding how we wanted to test our concept and getting into the scenario as a facilitator and role playing piece in the experience. For me, I had a lot of fun just coming up with the idea and dabbling with the constraint of bringing parents and children into the experience together. Service design is something I am definitely interested in, in the future.
